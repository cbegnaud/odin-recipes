Ingredients

  • 3 medium onions
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 3-4 cloves garlic
  • Seasonings of your choice
  • Vegetable oil for sautéing
  • 1 lb. Smoked sausage
  • 1 lb. Peeled and deveined Shrimp
  • Chicken stock or seafood stock about 5 to 6 cups

Instructions

  1. Cut up veggies and sauté until soft, do not brown
  2. Fry sausage and add to veggies
  3. Add stock and your seasonings
  4. Simmer at least 2-3 hours, the longer the better
  5. Then bring to a boil, add shrimp and cook for about 10 minutes
  6. Remove from heat and let sit about 10 minutes
